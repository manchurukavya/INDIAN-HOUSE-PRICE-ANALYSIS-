🏠 Indian House Price Analysis – Power BI Dashboard

🔍 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a large-scale Indian residential real estate dataset (250,000+ properties) using Power BI. The goal is to identify pricing trends, property distribution, and key factors influencing house prices to support real estate investment and business decision-making.

📊 Key Features of the Dashboard
🔹 KPI Cards:
Total Properties: 250K+
Average Price: ₹254.59 Lakhs
Avg Price per Sq Ft: ₹0.13 Lakhs
Property Types: Villa, Apartment, Independent House
🔹 Property Distribution:
Villas: ~33%
Independent Houses: ~33%
Apartments: ~34%
Balanced market distribution across property types
🔹 Price Analysis:
BHK-wise pricing remains consistent (~₹254–255 Lakhs)
Indicates BHK is not a major price driver
Other factors like location & amenities have more impact
🔹 Availability Analysis:
Ready-to-Move properties: ₹0.13L / Sq Ft
Under-Construction: ₹0.12L / Sq Ft
RTM properties slightly higher due to lower risk
🔹 City-Level Insights:
Bangalore has highest average price (~₹258L)
Top cities fall within ₹255–258L range
Shows price consistency across cities
🔹 Furnishing Analysis:
Furnished: ₹255L
Semi/Unfurnished: ₹254L
Slight premium for furnished properties
🔹 Interactive Filters:
State slicer
City filter
Property Type filter
BHK selection
Availability status filter
Fully dynamic dashboard

📈 Business Insights Delivered
Property prices are stable across BHK configurations
Location is the major price-driving factor
Ready-to-Move properties have higher demand and pricing
Market is evenly distributed across property types
Furnishing has minimal impact on pricing
Indian real estate shows price uniformity across major cities
